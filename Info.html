MARQET

Team members: Blake Vanniel, Blake Miller, Carlos Gonzalez, Mike Makdah, Will Youman Project Description: Marqet is a student to student website and application with the aim of simplifying shopping experiences on campuses all over the world. By using a simple user interface, students will be able to interact through both private messaging and community conversation boards. The initial goal will be to supply Marquette students with pages to exchange and sell textbooks and student basketball tickets. The end goal, however, is to branch-out into all types of items and as many universities as possible. We have not decided whether we want to base profit on a percentage taken out of sales or simply through advertisements in the future.
